# Utopia Raw — Brand Voice

**Purpose:** Keep product copy, email templates, and marketing content on-brand.

---

## The Promise

**Find Your Utopia.** The shower ritual itself is the product. Clean ingredients are table stakes — the differentiation is emotional. Utopia Raw is what you reach for at the end of a hard week, not a clinical skincare regimen.

## Aesthetic Lane

- **Reference anchor:** Travis Scott UTOPIA album art aesthetic (see `branding/references/`)
- **Empty lane claim:** warm-earth × dark-luxury × unisex. Nobody else is here. Not Rhode (dreamy nude), not Rahua (jungle green), not Nécessaire (clinical gray), not Saltair (ocean blue).
- **Not masculine, not feminine — unisex.** If copy reads as gendered, rewrite it.

## Voice Characteristics

- **Minimal.** One sentence is almost always better than three.
- **Sensory.** Name the smell. Name the feel. Name the temperature of the shower. Never abstract.
- **Unhurried.** Clean beauty is often rushed and clinical. Utopia Raw is slow.
- **First-person when possible.** "I built this" > "We built this." Tyler is the founder, and founder POV is the whole acquisition engine.

## Words to Use

- raw, ritual, shower, warm, earth, slow, unrefined, quiet, honest
- find, reach, breathe, end of the day
- scent names only if evocative (sandalwood, amber, cedar, fig, bergamot)

## Words to Avoid

- ❌ "luxurious" — show don't tell
- ❌ "pamper," "indulge," "treat yourself" — generic spa copy
- ❌ "revolutionary," "innovative," "breakthrough" — startup fluff
- ❌ "clean beauty" as a marketing term — just BE clean, don't talk about it
- ❌ "non-toxic" — legal minefield and cliché
- ❌ "dermatologist-tested" — wrong positioning; Utopia is artisan, not clinical
- ❌ Emoji in product copy (OK in social captions)

## Label Copy Format

Every product label follows this structure:

```
UTOPIA RAW                    ← wordmark
[Product Type]                ← Body Wash / Shampoo / Hair Mask
[Scent Name]                  ← Warm Sandalwood Amber (etc.)
Net Wt. 8 FL OZ / 236 mL      ← required by FDA
Ingredients: [INCI list]       ← required by FDA, alphabetized by %
Distributed by Tyler Youk      ← MoCRA requirement
[Address]                     ← MoCRA requirement
Made in USA                   ← only if formulator can confirm
FIND YOUR UTOPIA              ← tagline, small, bottom
```

Keep label copy silent on benefits, claims, or marketing language. Labels are functional. Marketing lives on the website.
